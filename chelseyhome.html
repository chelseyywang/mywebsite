Hello! My name is Chelsey Wang and I am a rising second year taking CS classes at UCLA and in the process of switching majors from mechanical engineering to computer science.
I am on the executive board of the Regents' Scholor Society at UCLA (RSS@UCLA), and I was also on the Electric Vehicle Rebuild Team in Bruin Racing.


what am i doing right now?
Currently, I am taking summer classes and teaching myself HTML+CSS+Javascript; as a RSS exec board member, I will be working on revamping and updating our website as well - rssla.org.


what did i do before college?
To be quite honest, computer science was definitely not on my mind before college, or even during the first quarter of college. I was more engrossed in figure skating--as I have been skating for 10 years.
I had been training as a singles skater, but in high school, I finally auditioned for the Los Angeles Ice Theater Senior Team, and made it onto the team. While I was on LAIT, we won three national bronze medals, and placed fifth at the international competition.
While skating as a team was a new, energizing and exciting experience, I still wanted to take the ice alone. So I entered the Solo Ice Dance Series, which had sectional competitions that skaters would join to earn a spot to go to nationals. My last year at nationals, I placed 5th in the Combined Event and 4th in the Shadow Dance event.
